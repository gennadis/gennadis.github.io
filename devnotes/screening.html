Screening

When prepping for tech interview screenings, it's smart to review common questions that pop up across backend and general programming interviews.

Focus on understanding the concepts, reasoning through problems, and explaining your thinking clearly.

Here are some great resources to guide your prep: Backend Interview Questions, Backend Developer Interview Questions, Python Interview Questions

Skim through them, but spend time actively thinking about each question. Practicing how you talk through your answers is what really makes a difference in tech interview screenings.
